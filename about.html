Who We Are?

Boss AR is a vibrant and distinctive clothing brand located in Purok Divisoria, Matuyatuya, Torrijos, Marinduque. The name "Boss AR" is derived from combining the first letters of the founders' names, Alden Eric Rosales and Rijay Rosales. This personalized touch not only reflects their shared vision but also adds a sense of identity to the brand. The founders' namesake contributes to the uniqueness of Boss AR, making it a representation of the creativity, style, and community spirit of Alden Eric Rosales and Rijay Rosales in the world of fashion.
